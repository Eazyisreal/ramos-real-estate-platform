FAQ

What is included in your property management service?

Our property management service delivers comprehensive care for your investment property. We handle everything from marketing and tenant placement to maintenance coordination and financial reporting, ensuring your property performs optimally while requiring minimal involvement from you. Our services include professional marketing, thorough tenant screening, regular inspections, and complete financial management.

How do you screen potential tenants

We take tenant screening seriously. Our process involves checking credit history, verifying employment and income, contacting previous landlords for references, and conducting background checks. This thorough approach helps us find reliable tenants who will care for your property and maintain timely payments.

What happens if a tenant doesn't pay rent?

When rent payment issues arise, we act swiftly. Our team immediately contacts late-paying tenants, issues formal notices if needed, and works to resolve the situation quickly. If necessary, we handle the entire eviction process through our experienced legal partners, protecting your interests every step of the way.

How do you handle maintenance requests?

For maintenance requests, we provide 24/7 emergency response and quick handling of routine repairs through our network of trusted contractors. All maintenance issues are addressed promptly, with emergency situations receiving immediate attention. We oversee all repairs and provide regular updates, ensuring your property stays well-maintained while controlling costs
